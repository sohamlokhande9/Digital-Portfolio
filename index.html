Who am I?

I'm a first-year Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student at MIT Academy of Engineering in Alandi, Pune. I am endlessly curious about what happens when hardware meets software. For me, engineering isn't just about reading textbooks; it's about getting hands-on, burning a few LEDs on a breadboard, and figuring out the logic that brings a microcontroller to life.

Beyond the tech, I believe creativity is just as important as logic. I'm deeply passionate about art, I play the tabla professionally (completed five classical tests), and I love being part of the Goonj Unplugged club. This blend of rhythm, art, and engineering shapes how I solve problems.

Whether I'm writing C++ or designing an IC 555 circuit, I approach every project with a builder's mindset.
